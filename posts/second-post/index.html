Project Globnet

In my tenure at CNK s.r.o., I contributed to the enhancement and upkeep of ORANGE’s optical cable network management system called Globnet. My role primarily involved upgrading and refining both the front-end and back-end components of this dynamic platform. This system is crucial for real-time monitoring and updating of cable network changes, including tracking damaged areas and managing the availability of free ports. I also played a role in problem-solving and optimizing system functionalities. This involved addressing various technical challenges, thereby improving the system’s overall efficiency and user experience.

This position was instrumental in honing my skills in full-stack development. It also provided me with experience in teamwork and adaptability within a fast-paced, technology-driven environment.
